How to choose the right therapy setting.

  • Postoperative rehab: choose a clinic comfortable with surgical protocols and communication with the surgeon.
  • Sports rehab: look for progressive strengthening, movement retraining and return-to-sport planning.
  • Balance/fall risk: balance-focused programs can be especially helpful for older adults.
  • Home health PT: useful for patients who medically qualify and cannot safely attend outpatient therapy.

What to bring to PT.

  • Therapy prescription or referral if required
  • Insurance cards and ID
  • Operative report or protocol if postoperative
  • Brace, sling, assistive device or home exercise plan
  • Comfortable clothing and supportive shoes

Need a PT referral?

Ask during your appointment which therapy setting best fits your diagnosis, procedure and activity goals.
