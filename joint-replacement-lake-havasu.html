Joint Replacement in Lake Havasu City

Hip, knee, and shoulder arthritis care in Lake Havasu City with shared decision-making about nonsurgical treatment, injections, surgical timing, and the safest procedure setting.

Joint replacement decisions should be clear, practical, and patient-centered.

Joint replacement is considered when arthritis, pain, stiffness, and loss of function remain limiting despite reasonable nonsurgical care. The right timing depends on symptoms, imaging, health, goals, and quality of life.

Common joint replacement evaluations

  • Hip arthritis and total hip replacement questions
  • Knee arthritis, robotic knee replacement, and partial vs total knee replacement discussions
  • Shoulder arthritis, anatomic shoulder replacement, and reverse shoulder replacement questions
  • Prior injections, therapy, medications, and activity modification that no longer help enough
  • Second opinions about timing, options, and surgical setting

Not every arthritis visit leads to surgery

Many patients benefit from education, therapy, weight and activity strategies, bracing, medications, injections, or time. Surgery is discussed when the condition, imaging, limitations, and patient goals line up.
