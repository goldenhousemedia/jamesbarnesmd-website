Sports medicine for the Havasu lifestyle.

Sports medicine is not only for elite athletes. It is for youth athletes, active adults, weekend warriors, retirees, and anyone trying to stay mobile around work, recreation, fitness, and family.

Common activities and injuries

  • Pickleball, golf, hiking, cycling, running, and gym injuries
  • Watersports, boating, wakeboarding, skiing, and PWC injuries
  • Motocross, UTV, desert sports, and fall-related injuries
  • Shoulder pain, rotator cuff tears, instability, and biceps problems
  • Knee pain, meniscus tears, ligament injuries, and swelling
  • Tendonitis, bursitis, sprains, strains, and overuse pain

Return-to-activity planning

Evaluation focuses on diagnosis, imaging when needed, pain generators, strength, motion, sport demands, and patient goals. Treatment may include physical therapy, bracing, injections, orthobiologics when appropriate, or surgery for selected injuries.
